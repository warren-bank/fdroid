<em>KeePassDX</em> allows editing encrypted data in a single file in <em>KeePass</em> format, and to fill in forms in a secure way.

Features:

<ul>
<li>Create database files, entries, and groups.</li>
<li>Support for .kdb and .kdbx files (version 1 to 4) with AES - Twofish - ChaCha20 - Argon2 algorithm.</li>
<li>Compatible with the majority of alternative programs (KeePass, KeePassXC, KeeWeb, …).</li>
<li>Allows opening and copying URI / URL fields quickly.</li>
<li>Biometric recognition for fast unlocking (fingerprint, face unlock, …).</li>
<li>One-Time Password management (HOTP / TOTP) for two-factor authentication (2FA).</li>
<li>Material design with themes.</li>
<li>Auto-Fill and Integration.</li>
<li>Field filling keyboard.</li>
<li>Dynamic templates.</li>
<li>History of each entry.</li>
<li>Precise management of settings.</li>
</ul>
