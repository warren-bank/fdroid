Conditional criteria include:

<ul>
<li>a whitelist of sender phone numbers</li>
<li>a substring that must occur at the beginning
of the received SMS message</li>
</ul>

GPS location data include:

<ul>
<li>latitude and longitude coordinates</li>
<li>accuracy (meters)</li>
<li>speed (meters/sec)</li>
<li>direction (cardinal or ordinal, and degrees)</li>
<li>URL to Google Maps</li>
</ul>
