Usage:

<ul>
<li>
displays a list of all paired Bluetooth devices

<ul>
<li>click the one that identifies the desired receiver</li>
<li>wait for connection</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
toggle the PTT button

<ul>
<li>green indicates that audio is currently being captured
from the microphone and streamed through the connected socket</li>
<li>red indicates that the audio stream is currently paused</li>
</ul>
</li>
<li>
move the slider to adjust the volume of the audio

<ul>
<li>default: 25%</li>
<li>maximum: 200%</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
