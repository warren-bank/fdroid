Features include:

<ul>
<li>Automatic discovery of AirPlay receivers within the same network</li>
<li>Beam photos from your device to AirPlay</li>
<li>Stream videos and music from your device to AirPlay</li>
<li>Mirror the screen from your device to AirPlay</li>
</ul>
